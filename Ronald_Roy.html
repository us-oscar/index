Welcome to the website of Prof. Ronald Roy's research group at OSCAR

We are an international research group located in Suzhou Industrial Park dedicated to both the fundamental  research on acoustics & optics and their clinical applications. Our key research interests lie primarily in a broad area, including Acousto-optic imaging, Multi-functional Contrast Agents, Bioeffects of Ultrasound & Cavitation, and Medical Ultrasound Imaging and Artificial Intelligence. We have three international research laboratories that are

Photo-acoustics and Acousto-optics Laboratory Industrial and Biomedical Acoustics Laboratory Medical Ultrasound Artificial Intelligence Laboratory
